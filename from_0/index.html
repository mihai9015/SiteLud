about us

The firm executes quality civil and industrial metal structures, of medium and small sizes.

We manage the process from preparing the documentation to assembly and even mounting, including preparation of the elements required for execution.

The client documentation is analyzed and processed before sending the project into the execution phase to avoid neclarities, material loss and spoilage.

The execution team (25 people) is composed of skilled personnel (welders, locksmiths, foreman) that respect the documentation provided by the engineer (execution drawings,)

Tools that are used during the manufacturing process include: plasma CNC, welding gear, bandsaw for cutting profiles, Linde S50 forklift, drilling and milling machines.
