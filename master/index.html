ABOUT US

The firm executes quality civil and industrial metal structures, of medium and small sizes. 

We manage the process from preparing the documentation to assembly and even mounting, including preparation of the elements required for execution. 

The client documentation is analyzed and processed before sending the project into the execution phase to avoid neclarities, material loss and spoilage.

The execution team (25 people) is composed of skilled personnel (welders, locksmiths, foreman) that respect the documentation provided by the engineer (execution drawings,)  
Tools that are used during the manufacturing process include: plasma CNC, welding gear, bandsaw for cutting profiles, Linde S50 forklift, drilling and milling machines,

RCS-RDS Radio Antennas Monopoles

Monopoles are polygonal sectioned and hot dip galvanized hollow steel structures. All accessories for on site assembly are bolted, consequent body sections are either slip jointed or bolted, base plates, flanges and accessories are shop welded to the sections. Structure for 200 pcs ( aprox 500 t)

Olympic ice rink 

An engineered system with built-in cost efficiencies that combined the economies of factory production with custom engineering design services and steel fabrication. Every building offered by the company is engineered independently—steel components are fabricated for the particular specifications of each project. Prefabricated steel buildings are delivered to the jobsite ready to put together.

Tennis court cover cupola

The manufacture of structures for prefabricated halls includes the supply of the backbone in several profiles – a truss, frame or tubular structure. Delivery includes steel columns, girders and rods.The basis of most contracts is the manufacture of structures, but, of course, we also produce equipment for prefabricated halls, such as staircases, handrails and flanges
